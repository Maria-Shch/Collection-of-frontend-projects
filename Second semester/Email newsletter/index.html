Training artificial intelligence

How clever is artificial intelligence? It currently helps us in many ways from car satnavs to detecting cancer cells but it's yet to be cleverer than us humans. We still need to train AI to do things but should we fear that it eventually learns too much? Neil and Sam discuss the subject and teach some useful vocabulary.

Facebook Boss apologises for data breaches

Mark Zuckerberg admits the company made mistakes and breached trust with its users.

Amazon begins appeal over Pentagon cloud contract

Amazon has filed an intention to appeal the US Department of Defense's decision to give a major contract to Microsoft.

Google taught the neural network to predict the weather eight hours ahead

Google Corporation introduced the neural network MetNet, which can predict the weather for eight hours ahead. The company claims that the algorithm can predict precipitation with an accuracy of one kilometer on the ground and up to two minutes in time.

YouTube downgrades default video quality worldwide by month

The YouTube video platform has decided to reduce the quality of default videos around the world due to the epidemic of the coronavirus COVID-19, writes Bloomberg. Changes to the site took effect on Tuesday, March 24 and will last 30 days.
